### T&D Gain Sharing — DISALLOWED (₹131.59 Cr):
**KSEBL's argument:** Actual 9.70% vs MYT target 10.82% → over-achievement
**Commission's logic:**
- Actual T&D loss in 2022-23 (trued up): **9.30%**
- Annual reduction target: **0.40%**
- Target for 2023-24: 9.30% - 0.40% = **8.90%**
- Actual 2023-24 (KSERC assessed): **9.76%**
- Loss INCREASED by 0.46% (from 9.30% to 9.76%), not decreased
- Under Reg 73(4), penalty should apply, but Commission WAIVED penalty due to extraordinary circumstances

### Regulatory provisions:
- Reg 73(3)(i): If actual loss > target → disallow PP cost for excess loss
- Reg 73(3)(ii): If actual loss < target → share savings 2:1 (licensee:consumer)
- Reg 14(1): Gains from controllable factors — 2/3 to licensee, 1/3 to consumers
- Annual loss reduction target: 0.40% per year
